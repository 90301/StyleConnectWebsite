Features

Style Connect features will allow you to better connect with your clients and save you time

  • Categorize Clients

    For easy search and quick retrieval of client information.

  • Scheduling

    Two schedule modes: Stylist view for a single user and front desk mode to allow for multiple Stylists and their schedules to be shown all on one screen.

  • Social Media Integration

    Style Connect integrates seamlessly with your favorite social media platforms.

  • Client Notes

    Notes section allows for Stylists to save info about their clients like their favorite styles so that they get the perfect haircut/style/color every visit.

  • Inventory Management

    Our software can help you manage your inventory and let you know when you are low on certain items. Alerts tell you when you are low on a particular item so you always have the right amount of inventory in stock.

  • Contact Reminders

    Style Connect software intelligently seeks and reminds Stylists of the optimal time to contact a client.
